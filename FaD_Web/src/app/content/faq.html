Do I need to give my location to search doctors in my locality? FINDaDOCTOR automatically captures your current location through geo-location feature while loading home page, you just need to choose a specialty and insurance to search doctors in your locality. You can change the location to search doctors in any other location. How do I search doctors who accepts my insurance plan? It’s easy, you need to choose an insurance provider in home page to see all the in-network doctors who accepts your insurance plan. I am a Patient, how can I sign-up/register with FINDaDOCTOR now? Thank you for showing interest to register with us. Soon we will enable sign-up/ registration features. How does FINDaDOCTOR safeguard my search information? FINDaDOCTOR is committed to protect privacy of user information, at FaD we take utmost care and consider every detail of data security to ensure patient personal information is safeguarded. FINDaDOCTOR has well defined security procedures that constantly monitor for any abnormalities in health information exchanges. Our secure storage and strong data encryption methods keeps your data secure.
