FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

we have put together a list of some questions to help you understand FINDaDOCTOR better.

Know more
How do I register with FINDaDOCTOR? 1. Go to https://www.findadoctor.com
2. Click on “Doctors Participate with us” button to go to provider’s page.
3. Click on “Join Us” to visit Providers SIGN UP page.
4. Enter the required information in the respective fields.
5. Create a password.
5. Read and Agree to the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
6. Click on the LIST YOUR PRACTICE button to verify your mobile number to register with us. How does listing my practice in FINDaDOCTOR add value to me? Currently, FINDaDOCTOR offers online doctor search features to healthcare consumers. Soon Team FINDaDOCTOR is coming up with more features which enable doctors to “Plan Your Availability” with date and time slot availability - Where Patients can search for nearby in-network insurance doctors and book online health appointments, “Preemptive Insurance Verification” - Insurance verified even before the patient visit the doctor for consultation and “PMS Integration” - Real-time sync for better scheduling. What security standards do you have in place to protect my information? FINDaDOCTOR has strong data security procedures that monitors information exchanges real-time and indicates any abnormalities. Our secure storage and strong data encryption methods keeps your data secure. As per federal laws only providers and their authorized representatives are eligible to create and manage account in FINDaDOCTOR. What is PROMOCODE-90? As a listed doctor, you will be automatically upgraded with 90 days of free subscription - PROMOCODE-90, once the “Plan Your Availability features” are enabled. How did you find my practice information? FINDaDOCTOR collects provider and practice information from various commercial and government sources such as NPI data. We have our standard data management practices to ensure that the information is presented in best way. We will ask providers/physicians to provide comprehensive details about their practice during signup/registration process. How often do you update my profile information in FINDaDOCTOR? FINDaDOCTOR updates data within one to three months based on the data updates from government and commercial sources. The information provided by you will be considered as accurate and latest. You can update your profile with recent additions by logging into your account. What is profile percentage? Profile percentage indicates the comprehensiveness of information provided in FINDaDOCTOR account. Information related to your education, board certifications, specialties, practice locations and insurances accepted, carries weightage in calculating profile percentage. It is always good to provide more details about your practice and increase profile percentage as our algorithm considers complete profiles to display top in the search results.
